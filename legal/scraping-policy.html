Scraping & Automated Access Policy

This policy governs how automated systems may access, read, index, copy, or process content published on BlankLabs.

TL;DR: Humans are welcome. Search engines are allowed. AI training, bulk scraping, and silent data harvesting are not.

1. Ownership of Content

All content on BlankLabs, including writing, images, project descriptions, metadata, and site structure, is protected by copyright and other applicable intellectual property laws.

This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Articles and long-form writing
  • Photography and visual assets
  • Project documentation and descriptions
  • Original layouts, sequencing, and presentation

2. Allowed Automated Access

The following automated access is permitted:

  • Major search engines indexing pages for discovery
  • Social media crawlers generating link previews
  • Accessibility tools operating on behalf of a human user

All permitted bots must respect robots.txt directives and standard crawl rate limits.

3. Explicitly Prohibited Uses

The following activities are strictly prohibited without prior written permission:

  • Scraping content for AI or machine learning training
  • Bulk downloading or mirroring of pages or assets
  • Dataset creation, embedding generation, or semantic indexing
  • Use of content in commercial, analytical, or surveillance systems
  • Bypassing technical measures intended to limit automation

Lack of a paywall does not imply consent.

4. AI Systems & Model Training

BlankLabs content may not be used to train, fine-tune, evaluate, or augment AI models, including large language models, image models, or multimodal systems.

This prohibition applies whether data is collected directly, indirectly, via third-party datasets, or through automated agents that ignore exclusion signals.

5. Enforcement & Consequences

If unauthorized scraping or automated access is detected, BlankLabs may take one or more of the following actions:

  • Immediate blocking of IP addresses, ASNs, or infrastructure
  • Formal abuse or takedown reports to hosting providers
  • Requests for deletion of collected data
  • Permanent placement on an internal bot blacklist

Repeated or willful violations may result in further legal or technical action.

6. Removal Requests & Permission

If your organization believes it has collected BlankLabs content in error, or wishes to request permission for automated access, you must contact us before continuing.

Email: blank@blanklabs.site

Requests must include:

  • The name of your organization or service
  • User agent(s) used
  • Purpose of access
  • Whether any data has already been collected

7. Relationship to robots.txt

This policy works alongside robots.txt. Ignoring exclusion directives does not override the restrictions defined here.

Automated access after publication of this policy constitutes notice.
